## Important testing boundary

Use fictional information and fictional documents only. Do not enter or send:

- Real medical or health information
- Real military, claim, or treatment history
- Social Security numbers or VA file numbers
- Another person's information
- Passwords, authentication codes, cookies, or private Google account screenshots

Debrief is independent, is not affiliated with VA, does not submit claims, and does not provide legal or medical advice. Alpha drafts and checklists may contain errors and must not be used for a real claim.

## Access

Sign in using the exact Google email address to which this invitation was sent. Because the application is in Google OAuth Testing mode, a different Google account may be denied access.

For this test round, a current version of Chrome or Safari on a desktop or laptop is preferred. Mobile and tablet feedback is welcome, but those experiences are still being evaluated. Please note your device and browser when providing feedback.

## What to test

1. Read the introduction and sign in.
2. Create a workspace using the provided fictional scenario.
3. Start a fresh claim and work through the questionnaire.
4. Review and edit the generated statement.
5. Save, leave, and resume the workspace.
6. Review/export the fictional claim package and identify what you would do next.

Please allow approximately 35–45 minutes for a full review. You may stop at any time.
